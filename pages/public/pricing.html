Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, and downgrades take effect at the end of your billing period.

What happens to my artworks if I downgrade?

Your existing artworks stay in your account. You just won't be able to add new ones until you're under your plan's limit.

How does AR viewing work?

Collectors scan a QR code or visit your gallery link. They can view any artwork in augmented reality at true-to-life scale on their walls.

Do collectors need to download an app?

No app needed. AR works directly in the browser on iOS Safari and Android Chrome. Collectors just tap "View in AR."

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards through our secure payment partner. Your payment details are never stored on our servers.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No contracts. All plans are month-to-month and you can cancel anytime. No hidden fees or cancellation charges.
