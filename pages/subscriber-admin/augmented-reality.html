Troubleshooting

  • 1
    Camera Permission Required

    Allow camera access when prompted. On iOS, you must tap "Start AR" first.

  • 2
    HTTPS Required

    AR requires a secure connection. Use localhost for testing or deploy with HTTPS.

  • 3
    Good Lighting

    Ensure the target image is well-lit and clearly visible. Avoid glare and shadows.

  • 4
    Supported Browsers

    Works best on iOS Safari and Android Chrome. Desktop browsers may have limited support.
